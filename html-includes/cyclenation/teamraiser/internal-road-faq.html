THE BASICS

CycleNation is a high-energy cycling event that’s empowering the nation to get healthier with good nutrition and regular cardio-exercise. At the same time, riders are raising funds to support the work of the American Heart Association/American Stroke Association. Our ambitious challenge? By 2020, to improve the cardiovascular health of all Americans by 20%, while reducing deaths from CVDs and stroke by 20%. (Challenge, Accepted.)

The American Heart Association and the American Stroke Association fund lifesaving research to help people live longer, healthier lives. Together we promote healthy living habits and awareness of heart disease and stroke, and advocate for heart and brain-healthy legislation.

SIGNING UP

You can sign up as a “Virtual Rider” and ride for the cause solo and close to home. You pick the place and time as long as it’s during the fundraising period (which ends one week after the event). All that’s required is your commitment to raise a minimum of $100.

There’s no registration fee, but "self-pledge" fee. It works like a "good faith" deposit toward your minimum fundraising goal. If that goal isn’t met, for any reason, within seven days after the Ride event, that shortfall will be charged to the credit card number you provided. For example: If your fundraising minimum is $100 and you raise only $50, after the fundraising period, your credit card account is charged $50. (The transaction will appear as BB AHA).

You can cancel your participation as a rider no later than 11:59 p.m. on Friday, March 2, 2018. To validly cancel your participation as a Rider, you must change your status on your RIDE profile and  contact us before the deadline. However, your self-pledge and any donations made to the American Heart Association on your behalf will not be refunded. All donations will be used to advance the AHA/ASA mission.

RAISING FUNDS

The minimum fundraising for a rider is $500. Getting there is easy! We have the tools you need to ask family, friends and co-workers to support your fight against stroke and heart disease. Your company may even have a match program to help you reach your goal. Once you sign up you will be amazed how our tools can help you reach and exceed your goal quickly.

If you don’t raise any money toward your fundraising minimum by the time the fundraising period expires (one week after the Ride ), the pledge fee you made at registration will be charged in full. Or, if you have raised some funds, you will be charged the difference between what you raised and the minimum amount.

Many employers sponsor matching gift programs and will match charitable contributions made by their employees. Check with your HR department to find out. If there is a company match, request a matching gift form from your employer and bring it to the event, completed and signed along with your donation, check or cash. Or, if you have donated online, we can pull the receipt for you. If your employer requires an Employer Identification Number (EIN), (that’s a Federal Tax Identification Number), use 13-561-3797.
