Fundraising with your mobile device

Download the mobile app by searching Jump/Hoops in the Apple App Store or Google Play

Share your progress on social media

You can use social media to quickly ask your social networks (Facebook, Twitter, etc.) to join your team or make a donation toward your fundraising efforts:

On your screen you will see five buttons after you log in. Choose the social media you would like to use and log in to that social media account

If you are already signed in to your social network, then you may not be asked to re-log into the site.

Fundraise with Facebook App

Download the Jump Rope & Hoops For Heart Facebook App and send messages to friends and family to support you!
